Privacy Policy - CashLoop

Last updated: June 2026

Use of the CashLoop app is also governed by our Terms and Conditions . We invite you to read them carefully alongside this privacy policy.

CashLoop respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user data. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data Collected

CashLoop does not collect personal data. All information entered by the user, including financial data, notes, and reports, remains exclusively on the device.

Specifically, the app does not collect, transmit, or share with third parties any of the following categories of data:

  • Identifying data
  • Contact data
  • User activity data
  • Location information
  • Content synchronized with external servers

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the device only. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data.

3. Third-Party Technologies

CashLoop does not use external services, SDKs, or third-party libraries to collect data, analytics, advertising, or tracking.

4. Required Permissions

The app requests only the permissions necessary for the features provided: access to the photo library to attach images to your transactions, access to files to export reports as PDF, and biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) to protect access to your data via the app lock.

5. User Rights

In accordance with GDPR, users may revoke permissions and remove data by uninstalling the app.

6. Security

CashLoop does not send data over the Internet and has no remote servers, minimizing the risks to data security.
